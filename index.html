IELTS
This is an English language test for study, migration or work. Over three million people take our test every year. IELTS stands for the International English Language Testing System (IELTS). IELTS is known and respected by teachers of English around the world. The test measures the language proficiency of those who want to work or study in countries where English is the native language spoken. This includes the UK, USA, Australia, New Zealand and Canada. IELTS is available at more than 1,600 locations worldwide. There are two IELTS tests a test taker can choose from. This includes IELTS Academic and IELTS General Training. Both test types assess the test taker’s ability in speaking, listening, reading and writing in English. Find out more about what's in the test.
